Maze V2

Maze V2 gameplay.

Project Status: Complete
Project Duration: 1 day
Software used: Unreal Engine 5.1
Logic: Blueprinting

A two-part tutorial from Reids Channel on Youtube helped me to create and complete this project. It's the second maze project I've done. Click here to see version 1!

This simple maze game drops a metallic marble onto a wooden board of obstacles. A glowing green goal is at the opposite corner of the board. The player uses the mouse/touchpad to tilt the board and guide the ball to the goal. The ball will respawn at the beginning of the board after reaching the goal.
