About Me

I'm a graduate of The Tech Academy through their C# and .NET bootcamp. I continued study with them to learn Unity, Unreal Engine, and the basics of game development. I'm looking to get my professional start in the game development industry! My main areas of interest are level design and logic programming. I'm always curious, love learning new things, and love being creative. Digital art is another area of interest for me, although moreso as a hobby. Incorporating my own art in my future video game projects is something that I look forward to!

Please feel free to contact me regarding any projects or even mutual interests that we share. I'd love to connect!
